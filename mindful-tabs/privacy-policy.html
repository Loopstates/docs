Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2025

Loopstates built the MindfulTabs extension as a Free product. This SERVICE is provided by Loopstates at no cost and is intended for use as is.

🛡️ Information Collection

The MindfulTabs extension operates entirely locally within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing information from our users.

All data related to your tab sessions, notes, and custom groups is stored exclusively on your local machine using the browser's local storage API. You are the sole owner and controller of your data.

🔑 Permissions Used

MindfulTabs requires the following system permissions to provide its core features:

  • "tabs": To organize and manage your active browser tabs.
  • "storage": To save your sessions and notes locally on your device.
  • "tabGroups": To create and color-code your tab groups.
  • "alarms": To trigger your scheduled wellness reminders.
  • "notifications": To alert you when it's time for a break.
  • "offscreen": To play non-intrusive audio for wellness alerts.
  • "<all_urls>": To allow navigation features and notes to persist across different websites.
