Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2025

Loopstates built the MindfulTabs extension as a Free product. This SERVICE is provided by Loopstates at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Information Collection and Use

The MindfulTabs extension operates entirely locally within your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing information from our users. All data related to your tab sessions, notes, and custom groups is stored exclusively on your local machine using the browser's local storage API.

Data Security

Since no user data is transmitted to our servers, the risk of external data breaches is eliminated. Your information remains on your device, under your control.

Permissions Used

MindfulTabs requires the following permissions to function:

  • "tabs": To access and manage your browser tabs for features like tab grouping, session saving, and focus mode.
  • "storage": To store your saved sessions, notes, custom groups, and extension settings locally on your device.
  • "tabGroups": To create and manage tab groups.
  • "alarms": To schedule and trigger wellness alarms and auto-close inactive tabs.
  • "notifications": To display system notifications for wellness alarms.
  • "offscreen": To play audio for wellness alarms in the background.
  • "<all_urls>": To allow the extension to interact with tabs across all URLs for features like tab notes and focus mode. This permission is necessary for the extension to function as intended across your browsing activity.
