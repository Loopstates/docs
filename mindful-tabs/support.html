Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is MindfulTabs?

MindfulTabs is a browser extension designed to help you organize your browser tabs, manage browsing sessions, stay focused, take notes, and receive wellness reminders.

💾 How do I save a browsing session?

Open the MindfulTabs popup, go to the "Sessions" tab, enter a name for your session, and click "Save Current Session."

🎯 How does Focus Mode work?

Focus Mode temporarily moves all other tabs in your current window to a new, minimized window, allowing you to concentrate on your active tab. When you exit, all tabs are restored instantly.

🔒 Are my notes and sessions stored online?

No. All your data (sessions, notes, custom groups, settings) is stored locally on your device using the browser's storage. We do not collect or transmit any of your browsing data.

⏰ How do I set up wellness alarms?

Go to the "Wellness" tab in the MindfulTabs popup. Choose an alarm type (e.g., Water Break, Stretch) and set an interval. Click "Add Alarm" to activate your reminder.

🗂️ How do I create custom tab groups?

In the "Grouping" tab, enter a name to create a new group. Then, click "Adjust Tabs" next to any group to select which open tabs you want to include or remove.
