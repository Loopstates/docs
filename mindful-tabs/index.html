Welcome to MindfulTabs!

MindfulTabs is your ultimate Chrome extension for a more organized and focused browsing experience. Developed by Loopstates, we bring you powerful tools to manage your digital life efficiently.

Key Features:

  • Tab Grouping: Create and manage custom groups for your tabs. Keep your workspace clutter-free and easily switch between contexts.
  • Session Manager: Save your entire browsing sessions and restore them anytime. Never lose your research or work setup again.
  • Focus Mode: Eliminate distractions by temporarily hiding other tabs, allowing you to concentrate on the task at hand.
  • Tab Notes: Jot down quick notes directly associated with specific tabs. Your notes reappear when you revisit the tab.
  • Wellness Alarms: Set customizable reminders for water breaks, stretches, eye exercises, and more, promoting a healthier browsing routine.

MindfulTabs is designed to enhance your productivity and well-being. Get started today and transform your browsing!
