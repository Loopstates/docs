Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is MindfulTabs?

A: MindfulTabs is a Chrome extension designed to help you organize your browser tabs, manage browsing sessions, stay focused, take notes, and receive wellness reminders.

Q: How do I save a browsing session?

A: Open the MindfulTabs popup, go to the "Sessions" tab, enter a name for your session, and click "Save Current Session."

Q: How does Focus Mode work?

A: Focus Mode temporarily moves all other tabs in your current window to a new, minimized window, allowing you to concentrate on your active tab without distractions. When you exit Focus Mode, your tabs are restored.

Q: Are my notes and sessions stored online?

A: No, all your data (sessions, notes, custom groups, settings) is stored locally on your computer using Chrome's local storage. We do not collect or transmit any of your personal browsing data.

Q: How do I set up wellness alarms?

A: Go to the "Wellness" tab in the MindfulTabs popup. You can choose an alarm type (e.g., Water Break, Stretch) and set an interval in minutes. Click "Add Alarm" to activate it.

Q: How do I create custom tab groups?

A: In the "Grouping" tab, you can create a new group by entering a name. Then, click "Adjust Tabs" next to a group to select which open tabs you want to include or remove from that group.

Q: What if I have a problem or suggestion?
