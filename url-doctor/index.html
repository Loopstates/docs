Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about operating with URL Doctor.

Can I use my own custom domain?
Absolutely. Even on our lower tiers, you can connect your own branded domain (e.g., link.yourbrand.com) to build trust and authority.
What happens if a destination URL breaks (404)?
Our "Self-Healing" technology detects the error and instantly redirects traffic to a safe fallback URL you define, so you never waste money on broken ads.
How is this different from standard affiliate tracking?
We give you full control. You can update destination URLs after you've sent them to influencers. Plus, our real-time analytics show you exactly which partner is driving revenue, not just clicks.
Can I expire a link after a specific date?
Yes. Perfect for limited-time offers. You can set a link to expire on "Black Friday at Midnight" and automatically redirect late clicks to a "Sorry, you missed it" page.
Do you sell my click data to third parties?
Never. Unlike free shorteners, you own 100% of your data. We are a privacy-first platform designed for businesses, not data brokers.
How do you stop bots from skewing my analytics?
Our "IP Shield" automatically filters out known scrapers, bots, and crawlers, ensuring your conversion data is accurate and you aren't paying for fake clicks.
Do you have an API for developers?
Yes. Everything you can do in the dashboard, you can do via our REST API. Automate link creation, retrieve analytics, and manage domains programmatically.
Do you have a mobile app?
We are working hard to bring more convenience to your pocket. A dedicated iOS and Android app is on our roadmap for Q4, allowing you to create and track links on the go.
How many smart routing configurations do you have?
We currently support over 12 distinct logic types (Device, Geo, Time, Language, etc.) as shown in the "Smart Routing" section above, and we are continuously adding more based on user feedback.
What kind of support do you offer?
We take support seriously. All users get access to our comprehensive help center and email support. Pro and Business plans include priority 24/7 chat support and a dedicated account manager for complex setups.
What happens to my links if I stop paying?
Your links stay active forever. You simply lose access to advanced features like "Smart Routing" and historical analytics. We never break your links until you completely migrate to something else and confirm us.
