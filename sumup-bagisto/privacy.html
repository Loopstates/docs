Privacy Policy

Protection and handling of data for SumUp for Bagisto.

Data Handling

The SumUp Payment Gateway for Bagisto extension acts as a secure bridge between your Bagisto store and SumUp processing. We prioritize transaction security.

  • Direct Processing: Transaction data is sent directly to SumUp. We do not store sensitive card details on your server.
  • Encryption: All communication between your server, the customer, and SumUp occurs over HTTPS.
  • Minimum Data: We only log essential transaction metadata for order synchronization and troubleshooting.

Third-Party Services

Payments are processed via SumUp. By using this extension, you are subject to the SumUp Privacy Policy.
