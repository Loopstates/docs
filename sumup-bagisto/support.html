Frequently Asked Questions

❓ The SumUp widget does not appear.

Ensure you have published the assets using php artisan vendor:publish --tag=sumup-assets and verify your API keys are correct for the active environment.

🚫 Orders are not being created after payment.

Check if your return URL is reachable and that the cart session hasn't expired. Verify your store's session configuration.

🔄 Does it support partial refunds?

Yes, you can initiate both full and partial refunds directly from the Bagisto admin order view.
